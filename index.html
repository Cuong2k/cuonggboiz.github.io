Edapt for leaders and teachers

By centralising data from all school systems, Edapt’s School Data Platform is a tool purpose built for school leaders and teachers.

It provides staff with a holistic view of every student across their attendance, behaviour and academic measures promoting rich conversations, earlier opportunities for intervention and better results all round.
